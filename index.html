Interesting facts about Chameleon

Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder!

Changing skin colors is an important part of communication among Chameleons!

Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree brances!

Interesting facts about Pigeon

Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals!

Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities!

Pigeons have excellent hearing ability!

Interesting facts about Camel

Camel's ears are furry!

When they find water, the will drink as much as possible!

Camels can move exceptionally well on the sand because of its specially designed feet!

Interesting facts about Cicada

Cicadas are the longest living insects with a lifespan of 4-17 years!

Periodical Cicadas remain underground and come out once every 17 years to mate!

The chirping sound made by the male Cicada is a mating call which a female can hear from over a mile (1.6 km) away!

Interesting facts about Panda

Pandas go from pink to black and white and even brown!

They can eat upto 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Pandas are extremely lazy but they can even swim!
